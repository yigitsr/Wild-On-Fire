Wild On Fire

Long Live Rock n Roll!

Wild On Fire is a Rock band from Turkey founded in 2015 by Ziggy Woxx (guitars) and Furkan Geckin (drums). Our motto is " Keep Rock n Roll alive ". We are influenced by the 70's and 80's Rock bands such as Deep Purple, KISS, Queen, Mötley Crüe, Van Halen , Dokken. We aim to bring back the true Rock n Roll played by our heroes to today. Current line-up : Goktug Karalti (vocals) , Yigit Sereflioglu (guitars) , Eren Engin (bass) , Furkan Gecking (drums) .
